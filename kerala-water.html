Kerala State Pollution Control Board Consultation (KSPCB)

Navigating the complexities of environmental regulations set forth by the Kerala State Pollution Control Board (KSPCB) can be challenging for businesses and industrial facilities. Compliance with local environmental laws is essential not only for legal adherence but also for promoting sustainable operations. This report outlines the consulting and advisory services provided to assist facilities in meeting KSPCB regulations, including air and water quality standards, solid waste management, and hazardous waste disposal.

KSPCB enforces a range of environmental regulations

It aimed at controlling pollution in Kerala.

Key areas include

  • Air Quality Standards
    • Regulations governing emissions from industries and vehicles.
  • Water Quality Standards
    • Guidelines for discharging wastewater into water bodies and maintaining safe drinking water standards.
  • Solid Waste Management
    • Protocols for the management and disposal of solid waste generated by industries and municipalities.
  • Hazardous Waste Disposal
    • Strict regulations regarding the handling, storage, and disposal of hazardous waste materials.
